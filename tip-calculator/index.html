How to Use the Tip Calculator

  1. Select your currency — choose USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, INR or PKR from the dropdown.
  2. Enter your bill amount — type the total shown on your receipt before tip.
  3. Choose a tip percentage — tap a quick button (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, 25%) or drag the slider for a custom amount.
  4. Enter the number of people — use the + / − buttons to split the bill evenly.
  5. View your results instantly — the tip amount, total bill, and per-person cost update live as you type.
  6. Copy, share or print — use the action buttons to save or send your results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I tip?

In the United States, a standard restaurant tip is 15 to 20 percent of the pre-tax bill for good service. In the UK, 10 to 12.5 percent is common and sometimes already included as a service charge. In Canada, 15 to 20 percent is typical, while tipping in Australia is optional since service staff are paid a full wage.

What is the standard tip in the USA?

The standard tip in the USA is 15 to 20 percent of the total bill before tax, with 20 percent generally considered generous and 18 percent a common default at restaurants.

How do I split a bill?

Enter the bill amount and tip percentage, then enter the number of people splitting the bill. The calculator divides the total bill including tip evenly and shows the exact amount each person owes.

Is tipping required?

Tipping is customary but not legally required in most countries. In the United States it is strongly expected in restaurants since many servers rely on tips as part of their income, while in countries like Japan and Australia tipping is optional.

How do I calculate a 20% tip?

Multiply the bill amount by 0.20. For example, a 50 dollar bill multiplied by 0.20 equals a 10 dollar tip, making the total bill 60 dollars.
